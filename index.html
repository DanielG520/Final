PAWsitive Difference is a foster-based rescue that saves dogs and cats from abandonment and shelter kill lists, then helps them find loving homes.

Get Involved

Our Mission

Our primary goal is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs and cats in need. We believe every pet deserves safety, veterinary care, and a family that will love them for life.

How We Help

PAWsitive Difference works with foster homes, local veterinarians, and volunteers to give animals a second chance. Pets live in real homes, not cages, while they wait for adoption.

We provide food, medical care, and support to adopters so each placement can truly be forever.

What you’ll find with PAWsitive Difference

Adoptable Pets

Meet dogs and cats of all ages and sizes looking for a second chance. Each pet has a story, a personality, and a foster family that knows them well.

Adoption Events

Join us at local pet stores and community events around the valley. It’s the easiest way to meet several adoptable pets in one visit.

Happy Tails

See updates from families who adopted through PAWsitive Difference. Their stories show what a big impact rescue can make in a pet’s life.

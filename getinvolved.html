Get Involved

PAWsitive Difference relies on fosters, volunteers, and donors to save lives. There’s a role for everyone — whether you open your home, give a few hours, or support medical care.

Ways to help

Foster a pet

Provide a temporary home, love, and basic care for a dog or cat until they’re adopted. We supply support and guidance along the way.

Volunteer your time

Help at adoption events, transport animals, or assist behind the scenes. Even a few hours a month can make a big difference.

Donate or sponsor

Your donations help pay for food, vaccines, spay/neuter, and emergency medical care for animals who have nowhere else to go.

Get Involved at a glance

Way to Help What it Involves Time Commitment
Foster Welcome a dog or cat into your home, provide daily care, and work with us to help them find the right adopter. From a few weeks to a few months, flexible
Volunteer Support adoption events, adoption paperwork, dog walking, or transport to vet appointments and foster homes. Weeknights and weekends as available
Donate Help cover medical bills, food, supplies, and special cases who need extra care or surgery. One-time or monthly
Host a drive Organize a supply or fundraiser at your school, workplace, or with friends to collect food, toys, or funds. Short-term project
